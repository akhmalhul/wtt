WWTP VERY IMPORTANT FOR TEXTILE

Due to the effects of water scarcity and stricter environmental regulations, the cost of fresh water utilization has increased worldwide. In addition, Textile industry is in urge to move toward sustainability, by reducing the consumption of water. The vast amounts of wastewater generated by textile processing facilities creates several challenges, meeting these challenges with sustainable treatment solutions, prevents water pollution from entering into our local watersheds protecting the public health and surrounding ecosystems. Utilizing a new approach through the use of advanced primary and secondary water treatment solutions is necessary to meet the challenges faced by the textile industry now and into the future. As companies in this industry move toward pursuing sustainability goals to reduce operational costs and comply with increasingly stricter regulations, it is becoming apparent that water reuse is one of the key component of these initiatives.
